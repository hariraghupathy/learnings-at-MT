Polymorphism:
  1.Compile-time polymorphism -> overloading
  2.Run-time polymorphism -> overriding

1.Overloading:
   ->The number of parameters may differ
   ->the type of parameters may differ
   ->return type may differ
   ->same name
   ->early binding
   ->same class 
   ->compile time polymorphism

2.Overriding:
   ->same signature
   ->same number of parameters 
   ->different class(by inheritance)
   ->late binding
   ->run time polymorphism
